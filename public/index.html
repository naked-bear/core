How it Works

1

Sign Up & Download

Create a free basic account and optionally upgrade to a standard or buisness account. Download the appropriate desktop application for your platform.

2

Add Files to Transfer

Browse for the files to be transferred. No need to upload files anywhere. You are the sole owner of your data.

3

Generate Secure URL

Secure URLs will automatically be generated once files are added. Set optional features like password protection, max download limit, etc.

4

Download Files

Files will be downloaded from your computer. NakedBear will relay the data between you and the downloader with end-to-end encryption.
